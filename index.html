Cherwin Benefit Auctioneers

Cherwin Auctioneers, headed by Barry and Ric Cherwin, is a Fund Raising Auction company specializing in Benefit Auctions. With heart and humor, exuberance and style, Cherwin Benefit Auctioneers are expert at tapping into the critical emotional tenor at any charitable event. Employing motivational techniques and pragmatic solutions honed over hundreds of live auctions, The Cherwin Brothers inspire otherwise reluctant and passive audiences to abandon caution, open their wallets, save lives, protect the environment and financially support with welcome enthusiasm, the very cause that motivated them to attend in the first place.

Ric and Barry

Ric Cherwin is known as the singing auctioneer. He is also a concert producer, musician, composer, recording artist, writer, former Daily News editor, practicing psychotherapist and attorney, among his various pursuits. Brother Barry has been New York Bid Caller and Auctioneer of the Year. Not only in demand as auctioneers, Ric and Barry Cherwin are also consultants and speakers who conduct a multitude of seminars and webinars centered upon cause-related benefits and auctions. Their presentation, “10 Keys to Holding Successful Fundraising Auctions,” has helped organizations garner previously untapped monies and to cumulatively generate millions from auctions conducted by the Cherwin Brothers. To learn more about Ric check out his personal website.

Partnerships

Cherwin Benefit Auctioneers provide services for major medical foundations (JDRF, ALS, AHA), foundations (Doctors Without Borders, World Wildlife Fund, African Rainforest Conservancy, SPCA), scores of local and national fund-raising organizations, as well as school, art and estate auctions. Ric has served as auctioneer for the South Asia Excellence Awards show, televised to over 100 million people throughout Europe and Asia. Their auctions are known for their energy, joie de vivre, pace, warmth and for being very lucrative for the sponsoring organizations, regularly raising more than the organization ever did before. The brothers have worked with scores of major celebrities at their gala fundraisers, to name just a few: President Bill Clinton, Patti LaBelle, Michael Douglas, Judy Collins, more than a dozen current and former Yankees and Mets, Knicks and Nets, all of whom sing the praises of Barry and Ric’s work and ability to raise money for their causes .
